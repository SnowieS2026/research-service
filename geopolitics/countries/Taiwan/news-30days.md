## 🛡️ Taiwan's Defence — Building Asymmetric Capabilities (March 2026)

### TSMC and Taiwan's Silicon Shield (March 2026)
- **Taiwan produces ~92% of the world's most advanced semiconductors** — the most critical technology in the global economy.
- **TSMC** — the world's most advanced chip foundry — producing 3nm and 2nm chips for Apple, Nvidia, AMD, Qualcomm.
- This is Taiwan's **ultimate deterrent** — China attacking Taiwan would disrupt global chip supply, devastating the world economy including China itself.
- TSMC building **Arizona and Japan fabs** — diversifying away from Taiwan but maintaining cutting-edge production in Taiwan.

### Taiwan's Military Modernisation — From Heavy Forces to Precision Missiles (March 2026)
- Taiwan buying **F-16V jets, M1A2 tanks, and Harpoon anti-ship missiles** from the US.
- Taiwan developing **Hsiung Feng anti-ship missiles** — indigenous supersonic cruise missiles.
- **Kuo-li-ne (Taiwan submarines)** — indigenous submarine fleet being built — first ones operational 2025-2027.
- Taiwan introducing **mandatory national service for women** — a 4-month military training requirement.

## 🇨🇳 China Relations — Beijing's Pressure Campaign (March 2026)

### China's Air and Naval Encirclement (March 2026)
- **PLA Air Force crossing the Taiwan Strait median line daily** — Taiwan scrambling fighters.
- **China's aircraft carrier Taiwan-strat exercises** — simulating a blockade.
- Taiwan's **ADIZ (Air Defence Identification Zone)** breached dozens of times monthly by Chinese aircraft.
- China's **coast guard and "gray zone" operations** — using civilian vessels to test Taiwan's response without triggering escalation.

### Economic Coercion — Chinese boycotts of Taiwanese goods (March 2026)
- **China periodically banning Taiwanese imports** — pineapples, fish, beer — to punish Taiwan politically.
- Taiwan diversifying export markets — **US and Southeast Asia** becoming more important as China shrinks Taiwan's export share.
