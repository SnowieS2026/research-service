## 🛢️ Energy — Norway's Gas Exports and the Middle East Crisis (March 2026)

### Norway's Gas Exports to Europe — Critical Ally (March 2026)
- **Norway has replaced Russia as Europe's largest gas supplier** — a transformative shift since 2022.
- Norway now supplying **~120-130 billion cubic metres of gas annually** to Europe — directly replacing Russian pipeline gas.
- The **Strait of Hormuz crisis** (Iran threatening to block the passage) has made Norway even more strategically valuable — Europe is more dependent on Norwegian gas if Middle East routes are disrupted.
- Norway extracting record volumes from the **Johan Sverdrup** oil field and the ** Troll** gas fields.

### Norway's Sovereign Wealth Fund — $1.7 Trillion (March 2026)
- **The world's largest sovereign wealth fund** — worth ~$1.7 trillion, built on oil revenues.
- Norway's Government Pension Fund Global investing globally — with strict ethical screens.
- The fund owns **~1.5% of all global listed stocks** — making Norway a significant shareholder in companies worldwide.

## 🐟 Norway's Seafood Industry — Competing with Russia (March 2026)

### Russian Counter-Sanctions — Norwegian Salmon Blocked (March 2026)
- **Russia banned Norwegian salmon imports** in 2014 and has maintained the ban — Norway has had to find alternative markets.
- China and the US have become major markets for Norwegian salmon — Russia a small fraction of Norwegian seafood exports now.
- Russia using **seafood as a political weapon** — Norwegian fishing communities have been damaged but Norway has diversified.

## 🇸🇯 Svalbard — Norway's Arctic Paradox (March 2026)

### Svalbard Treaty — Norway's Constrained Sovereignty (March 2026)
- **Svalbard** — the Norwegian Arctic archipelago — governed by the 1920 Svalbard Treaty.
- All signatories of the treaty have **equal rights to commercial activities** in Svalbard — including Russia, which maintains a presence.
- Russia operating near Svalbard — monitoring Norwegian and NATO activity in the High North.
