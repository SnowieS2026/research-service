## 🔴 Breaking / Major Stories

### Germany Will Send Taurus Missiles to Ukraine (March 2026)
- **Chancellor Friedrich Merz has confirmed Germany will send its long-range Taurus cruise missiles to Ukraine** — capable of striking targets deep inside Russia, including Moscow.
- This is a **major policy reversal** — previous SPD-led government blocked Taurus deliveries over fears of escalation.
- Merz's reasoning: Ukraine needs to be in the **strongest possible position** at the negotiating table — Taurus gives Ukraine a significant military capability boost.
- UK and France already sending Storm Shadow/SCALP long-range missiles to Ukraine.
- **Russia condemned the decision** — warning that Taurus missiles would be considered a direct NATO escalation.

### Ukraine-Russia Ceasefire Talks Underway (March 2026)
- **Putin and Trump held direct talks** — discussing a proposed 30-day ceasefire agreement.
- **Ceasefire talks being held in Turkey** — Istanbul serving as the diplomatic venue.
- **Germany and France cautioning against accepting a ceasefire** that leaves Russia in control of occupied Ukrainian territory.
- **Russia now controls most of the Kursk region** — Ukrainian forces that briefly occupied Russian territory have been largely pushed back by Russian counter-offensives.
- This shifts the negotiating dynamic — Russia regaining territory gives Putin more leverage at the ceasefire talks.

### Germany's New Government — Merz Leads Coalition (February–March 2026)
- **CDU/CSU leader Friedrich Merz** became Chancellor following the February 2025 election — forming a coalition government.
- Merz has signalled a more **assertive German foreign policy** — positioning Germany as Europe's leading power alongside France.
- **Merz warned: "Germany will be unreliable for Europe"** if the SPD wins — attacking the SPD's more cautious approach to Ukraine and Russia.

## 🇺🇦 Russia-Ukraine War Support

### German Military Aid to Ukraine (2022–2026)
- **Germany is Europe's largest donor of military aid to Ukraine** — €28B+ in total support since 2022.
- **Zeitenwende declared** — Germany committed to 3% of GDP on defence by 2030 (a historic shift from post-WWII pacifism).
- **Taurus missiles** now confirmed for delivery — Germany joining the UK's Storm Shadow programme in providing long-range strike capability to Ukraine.
- **Büchel air base** — Germany hosts ~20 US B61 nuclear bombs, part of NATO's nuclear sharing arrangement.

## 🛢️ Energy — Germany Pivot Away from Russia

### Germany Reduced Russian Gas From 55% to Under 5% (2022–2026)
- One of the most dramatic energy policy shifts in modern history — **Germany has cut Russian gas imports from 55% of supply to under 5%** since 2022.
- LNG terminals built at breakneck speed — replacing Russian pipeline gas with US, Norwegian, and Qatari LNG.
- **Germany now Europe's largest LNG importer** — though this has increased energy costs for German industry.
- Energy transition accelerating — wind, solar, and hydrogen investments surging.
