### Iran's War with Israel — Impact on Australia (March 2026)
- **Iran-Israel conflict (March 2026):** A major regional war erupted in early-to-mid March 2026 following Israeli strikes on Iranian nuclear facilities. Australia has been directly impacted:
  - **Fuel prices surged sharply** — diesel and petrol prices hitting Australian households and businesses. The Coalition called for emergency fuel excise cuts.
  - **Diesel costs blowing out infrastructure projects** — industry groups warned that infrastructure builds are stalling due to fuel cost overruns.
  - **Oil dependency crisis** — Australia's dependence on imported packaging and oil exposed. Renewed talks of levies on plastic packaging and domestic refining capacity.

  - **Qantas rerouting flights** — Qantas shifting Europe routes to avoid Middle East airspace. The "kangaroo route" (Australia to Europe via Middle East) being restructured.
