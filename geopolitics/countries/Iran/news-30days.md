## 🔴 Breaking / Major Stories

### US-Israel Strikes on Iranian Nuclear Facilities — War Begins (February 28, 2026)
- **On February 28, 2026, Israel — with US support — launched strikes on Iranian nuclear facilities**, marking a dramatic escalation from the shadow war into direct military conflict.
- Israeli F-35 jets struck multiple sites including the **Natanz uranium enrichment facility** and the **Arak heavy water reactor**.
- **Iran's nuclear programme set back by years** — but Iran retains enough enriched uranium for multiple bombs if it chooses to pursue them.
- Iran's Supreme Leader ** Ayatollah Khamenei declared a national emergency** — signalling Iran would retaliate.

- Iran's strategy: **use the threat of oil disruption as a deterrent** — making it clear that any further strikes will be met with catastrophic response.

### Iran's Regional Proxy Network — Hezbollah, Houthis, Iraqi Militias (March 2026)
- **Iran activated its regional proxy network** — Hezbollah in Lebanon, Houthi rebels in Yemen, and Iraqi Shia militias all on heightened alert.

## ☢️ Nuclear Programme — Iran's Breakout Capability

### Iran's Nuclear Status (March 2026)
- **Iran enriched uranium to 60% purity** — a level just below weapons-grade (90%).
- After the Israeli strikes, Iran announced it was **removing all remaining limits** on its nuclear programme.
- **IAEA inspectors expelled** from Iran — making verification of Iran's programme impossible.
- Iran's breakout time: **estimated at 2-3 weeks** to produce enough weapons-grade material for a nuclear bomb.
- Iran has **multiple enrichment sites** — Natanz, Fordow, and others — making a US/Israeli military "bloody nose" strike nearly impossible to neutralise completely.

### Iran's Nuclear Doctrine — Khatam Anbiya (March 2026)
- Iran's nuclear programme is managed by the **Khatam Anbiya** headquarters — the largest economic conglomerate in Iran, controlled by the IRGC.
- Khamenei has issued a **fatwa (religious ruling) declaring nuclear weapons haram** (forbidden) — but analysts believe this is a strategic ambiguity policy.
- If Iran crosses the nuclear threshold, it would be the **first new nuclear-armed state since North Korea in 2006**.

## 🛢️ Oil Markets — Iran's Leverage

### Strait of Hormuz — The Nuclear Option (March 2026)
- **20% of global oil and LNG passes through the Strait of Hormuz** — Iran has threatened to close it.
- If Iran follows through, **oil prices surge above $200/barrel** — a global economic shock.
- The US would likely respond militarily to keep the strait open — risking direct US-Iranian naval combat.
- **China, Japan, South Korea, and India** — all heavily dependent on Middle East oil — are the most exposed.
- This is **Iran's ultimate deterrent** — and the reason the US and Israel have been reluctant to strike Iranian nuclear facilities until now.
