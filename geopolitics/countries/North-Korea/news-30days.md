## 🔴 Breaking / Major Stories

- **North Korean troops are taking heavy casualties** — Ukrainian forces have killed hundreds of North Korean soldiers.
- This represents a **massive strategic escalation** — a new nuclear-armed state openly fighting in the Russia-Ukraine war.
- **Kim Jong-un is receiving military technology in exchange** — Russia sharing nuclear submarine, missile, and spy satellite technology with North Korea.
- This is a **transformative development** — Russia violating its own international isolation to give North Korea advanced weapons technology.
