## 🏛️ Erdoğan's Turkey — Twenty Years of AKP Rule (March 2026)

### President Erdoğan — Still Dominating Turkish Politics (March 2026)
- **Recep Tayyip Erdoğan** has ruled Turkey since 2003 (as PM then President) — 22 years in power.
- His **AKP (Justice and Development Party)** remains the largest party — but economic crisis and youth dissent are eroding support.
- Erdoğan's **conservative Islamic democracy** — blending Islam and democracy, NATO membership, and EU bid.
- **Turkey's EU accession process frozen** — but Turkey remains a crucial migration management partner for the EU.

### 2028 Turkish Presidential Election — Erdoğan's Last Stand? (March 2026)
- Erdoğan will be **74 in 2028** — speculation about succession within the AKP.
- **CHP (Republican People's Party)** — secular, Atatürk's party — winning municipal elections in Istanbul and Ankara in 2024.
- Turkey's **Kurdish question** — the HDP/PKK conflict ongoing, with periodic peace negotiations.

## 💰 Economy — Chronic Crisis and Inflation (March 2026)

### Turkey's Inflation and Currency Crisis — Erdoğan's Unorthodox Economics (March 2026)
- **Turkey has experienced severe inflation** — prices surging 100%+ in 2022-2023, finally declining.
- Erdoğan's unorthodox view that **high interest rates cause inflation** — instead of the conventional view — led to policy paralysis.
- Turkey's **central bank finally raising rates** — under new Finance Minister Şimşek's orthodox programme.
- **Currency (lira) has lost 80%+ of its value** against the dollar since 2018 — Erdoğan's policies have crushed purchasing power.
- Turkey receiving **IMF conditional assistance** — Şimşek's programme of fiscal austerity in exchange for credibility.
