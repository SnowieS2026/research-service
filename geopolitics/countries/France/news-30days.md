## 🇪🇺 European Strategic Autonomy

### France's Nuclear Modernisation — Protecting French Deterrence (March 2026)
- France continuing to modernise its **force de frappe** — the independent nuclear deterrent.
- **PANG programme** (Porteur de Nouvelle Génération) — France's next-generation nuclear submarine, set to replace the current Triomphant class by 2038.
- Macron emphasising France's unique role as Europe's only truly independent nuclear power — and therefore the backbone of European strategic autonomy.

### Franco-German "Engine" — Tensions Under Stress (March 2026)
- The Franco-German relationship showing strain over Russia/Ukraine policy — Germany more cautious about supplying long-range missiles to Ukraine.
- France pushing for more aggressive support to Ukraine; Germany more cautious.
- Despite tensions, both countries committed to the EU project — and cooperating on EU defence industrial policy.

## 🛡️ Defence & Military

### Operation Barkhane Officially Ended — France's Sahel Exit Complete (February 2026)
- **France formally ended Operation Barkhane** — the decade-long counter-terrorism mission across the Sahel region of Africa.
- France has withdrawn from Niger, Mali, Burkina Faso, Chad, and other Sahel states — expelled following military coups that turned toward Russia (Wagner/group).
- **France pivoting to coastal West Africa** — new security partnerships with Gulf of Guinea states (Ivory Coast, Senegal, Benin).
- France still maintaining military presence in Djibouti (French Indian Ocean base) and the Central African Republic.

### French Defence Exports — Major Deals (March 2026)
- France continuing to market its defence industry globally — **Rafale fighter jets**, **Mistral warships**, **SCALP cruise missiles**.
- Major customers: India, UAE, Qatar, Egypt, Indonesia — France competing with US and Russian arms industries.
