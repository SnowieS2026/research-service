## 🇺🇸 US-Singapore Relations — Strategic Partnership (March 2026)

### CHIPS Act and Singapore — Attracting Semiconductor Investment (March 2026)
- **Singapore attracting massive semiconductor investment** — Micron, GlobalFoundries, and others expanding Singapore operations.
- The US **CHIPS Act has indirect effects on Singapore** — companies receiving US subsidies are relocating some production from Singapore to the US.
- Singapore negotiating an **Critical Minerals Agreement** with the US — similar to what Japan and the EU have — to access CHIPS Act benefits.
- **Singapore hosting US military assets** — the Singaporean Armed Forces training with US forces, access torepair and resupply facilities for US Navy.

## 🛢️ Energy and the Strait of Malacca

### Singapore's Energy Vulnerability (March 2026)
- **The Strait of Malacca** — the world's most important chokepoint, carrying 16% of global trade.
- Singapore's **energy security strategy**: diversify import sources (LNG from Qatar, UAE, US; oil from Middle East and Russia).
- **Jurong Island** — the world's largest exporter of refined petroleum products from a single location.
- Singapore **strategic oil reserves** — government-mandated stockpiles for 90+ days of consumption.
