## 🇮🇷 Iran — Saudi Arabia's Strategic Calculation (March 2026)

### Saudi-Iran Rapprochement at Risk (March 2026)
- **Saudi Arabia and Iran restored diplomatic relations in 2023** — a significant de-escalation after years of proxy war.
- That rapprochement is now **under severe strain** — Iran's war with Israel and Saudi Arabia's intercepted Iranian strikes threaten the détente.
- However, Saudi Arabia is **careful not to provoke Iran** — aware that a direct Saudi-Iran confrontation would be catastrophic.
- **Saudi strategy: contain the conflict** — provide diplomatic cover for de-escalation without being seen as pro-Israel.
