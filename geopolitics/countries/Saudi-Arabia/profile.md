## 2. Leadership & Politics

### Monarchy Structure

- **King & Prime Minister:** Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud (b. 1935, reigned since 2015)
- **Crown Prince & De Facto Ruler:** Mohammed bin Salman (MBS, b. 1985)
- **Governance Model:** Absolute monarchy with Quran as constitution; no political parties or elected legislature
- **Succession:** Changed from agnatic seniority to vertical succession by King Salman (2017), consolidating power in MBS

### Mohammed bin Salman (MBS)

- **Age:** ~40 (born August 31, 1985)
- **Position:** Crown Prince, Prime Minister, Chairman of Council of Economic and Development Affairs, Minister of Defense
- **Key Initiatives:** Vision 2030, NEOM megaproject, Saudi Green Initiative, anti-corruption purge (2017), social liberalization
- **Power Consolidation:** Systematically removed rival princes (Al-Nayef, Al-Muqrin), centralized control over security apparatus, National Guard, and media

### Domestic Reform Program

- **Social Changes:** Women allowed to drive (2018), cinemas reopened, entertainment sector deregulated, restrictive male guardianship laws relaxed
- **Economic Reforms:** Foreign investment encouragement (PIF sovereign wealth fund), Aramco partial privatization (2019, $29.4B IPO), tourism sector development
- **Anti-Corruption Campaign (2017):** Detained 381 individuals at Ritz-Carlton Riyadh; recovered ~$107 billion in settlements
- **Controversies:** Jamal Khashoggi assassination (Istanbul, 2018), crackdown on dissidents, extensive surveillance state
