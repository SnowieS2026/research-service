## 🔴 Breaking / Major Stories

### UAE and the Iran-Israel War — Gulf Frontline (March 2026)
- **The UAE is directly in Iran's firing line** — Iranian missiles and drones have targeted UAE territory (Abu Dhabi, Dubai) as part of Iran's retaliation against Israel.
- **UAE air defences intercepting Iranian strikes** — American-made THAAD and Patriot batteries protecting UAE skies.
- The **Strait of Hormuz** is existential for the UAE — 20% of global oil transits through it; any Iranian blockade devastates UAE oil exports.
- UAE coordinating intensively with the **US military** — Al Dhafra air base hosting advanced US aircraft (F-35s, B-52s).
- UAE extracting itself from the **Abraham Accords backlash** — normalising with Israel was politically costly at home.

### UAE and the Israel-Hamas War — Diplomatic Tightrope (March 2026)
- UAE condemning Hamas's October 7 attack — but also calling for humanitarian ceasefire in Gaza.
- **UAE was the first Arab state to normalise with Israel** (2020, Abraham Accords) — now paying a political price domestically.
- UAE continuing de facto relations with Israel but **no public diplomatic escalation** while Gaza is devastated.

## 🛢️ Oil and the OPEC+ Strategy — UAE Benefiting from Crisis (March 2026)

### OPEC+ Compliance — UAE Deficit Cheating (March 2026)
- **UAE producing ~3.5 million barrels/day** — exceeding its OPEC+ quota, cheating on production limits.
- UAE benefiting from **elevated oil prices** ($90-100/barrel due to Iran crisis) — filling state coffers.
- **Abu Dhabi's ADNOC** — state oil company investing heavily in downstream and petrochemicals.

### UAE as a Global LNG and Oil Hub (March 2026)
- **The UAE has positioned itself as a global energy trading hub** — competing with Singapore for Asian LNG and oil customers.
- UAE importing **Russian LNG** (re-exported, sanctions-busting) and selling to Europe and Asia.
- ADNOC expanding in **renewables** — investing in solar (Masdar) and nuclear (Barakah nuclear plant with Korea).

### UAE's Independent Foreign Policy — Different from Saudi Arabia (March 2026)
- **The UAE is more independently minded than Saudi Arabia** in some areas — closer to the US, less ideologically committed to Palestinian cause.

## 🏙️ Dubai — The Global Business Hub (March 2026)

### Dubai's Economy — Post-Pandemic Boom (March 2026)
- **Dubai's economy is overheating** — property prices up 30%+ in 2024-2025, rental costs surging.
- **Russian money flowing into Dubai** — oligarchs and companies relocated from sanctions-hit Russia.
- **Chinese capital also flooding Dubai** — as US-China tensions increase, Chinese firms using Dubai as an export hub.
- Dubai emerging as the **world's 4th busiest airport** — after London, New York, and Beijing (for transit).
- **Gold and commodities trading** — Dubai's DGCX and gold souks making it a commodities hub.

### Abu Dhabi vs Dubai — Two Emirs, Different Styles (March 2026)
- **Abu Dhabi holds 95%+ of UAE's oil reserves** — wealthier per capita but quieter than Dubai.
- **Abu Dhabi's sovereign wealth funds (Mubadala, ADIA, ADQ)** — collectively managing ~$1.5 trillion — investing globally.
- **Abu Dhabi's ADNOC** — third-largest integrated energy company in the world.
