## 💰 Swiss Banks and the End of Banking Secrecy (March 2026)

### Automatic Exchange of Tax Information — CRS (March 2026)
- **Switzerland implementing the OECD Common Reporting Standard (CRS)** — automatic exchange of financial account information with 100+ countries.
- **Swiss banking secrecy effectively dead** for tax purposes — foreign tax authorities can now identify Swiss bank account holders.
- Switzerland still a major wealth management centre — but now more transparent than its reputation suggests.

### Swiss Banks and US Sanctions — Compliance Pressure (March 2026)
- **UBS and Credit Suisse** (now merged) — complying with extensive US and EU sanctions on Russia, Iran, and others.
- Swiss banks still holding **significant Russian wealth** frozen under sanctions — the subject of ongoing legal and political debate.

- Switzerland's **armed neutrality** — independent defence capability, including a neutralised army capable of mountain warfare.
