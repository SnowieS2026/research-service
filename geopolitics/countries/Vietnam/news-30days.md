## 🇨🇳 China-Vietnam Relations — The Northern Neighbour (March 2026)

### China-Vietnam Land Border — Normalising (March 2026)
- **China and Vietnam share a 1,280km land border** — historically a source of conflict but now stable.
- **China is Vietnam's largest trading partner** — ~35% of Vietnam's exports go to China (textiles, electronics, agricultural products).
- Vietnam's **electronics exports to China growing** — as Vietnamese factories process Chinese components for re-export.
- However, **Vietnam is deeply wary of Chinese political influence** — suppressing pro-China voices in civil society.

### South China Sea — Vietnam's Most Dangerous Flashpoint (March 2026)
- **Vietnam is one of 6 claimants in the South China Sea** — the others: China, Philippines, Malaysia, Brunei, Taiwan.
- **Vietnam has been physically contesting Chinese claims** — multiple naval confrontations near the Spratly and Paracel Islands.
- Vietnam has **boosted its naval budget** — buying submarines (Kilo-class from Russia), corvettes, and anti-ship missiles.
- **Chinese coast guard ships sinking Vietnamese fishing vessels** — regular incidents in disputed waters.
- Vietnam seeking **international arbitration** against China's claims — but China ignores rulings.

## 🏭 Economy — Vietnam's Manufacturing Boom (March 2026)

### Vietnam as China's Alternative Manufacturing Base (March 2026)
- **Vietnam is the poster child for China+1 strategy** — Apple, Samsung, Intel, LG all manufacturing in Vietnam.
- Samsung makes **~50% of its smartphones in Vietnam** — the largest single foreign investor.
- **Apple moving some MacBook and iPad production to Vietnam** — Foxconn and Pegatron facilities.
- Vietnam's **GDP growth ~6-7% annually** — one of the fastest growing economies in the world.
- **Vietnam's FDI (foreign direct investment) at record highs** — $18-20B annually flowing in.

### Vietnam's Challenges — Infrastructure and Education (March 2026)
- **Infrastructure bottleneck** — Vietnam's roads, ports, and power grid struggling to keep up with manufacturing boom.
- **Power shortages** — Vietnam facing electricity rationing in some regions; Samsung and others facing load shedding.
- **Vietnam's workforce is relatively low-skilled** — limited English, limited engineering talent at scale.
- Vietnam aiming to move up the value chain — **from assembly to components** — mirroring China's path.

## 🛢️ Energy — Vietnamese Dependency on Coal and Imports (March 2026)

### Vietnam's Power Crisis — Coal, Hydro, and LNG (March 2026)
- **Vietnam still heavily dependent on coal** — coal-fired power stations being built (with Chinese financing) to meet surging demand.
- Vietnam exploring **LNG imports** — building LNG terminals to transition from coal.
- **Vietnam's electric vehicle market growing** — VinFast (Vietnamese) and Chinese EV brands competing.
