## 🔴 Breaking / Major Stories

### Catastrophic Floods in Minas Gerais — 64+ Dead (Late February 2026)
- Heavy rains triggered floods and landslides across southeastern Brazil, primarily in **Minas Gerais state** — one of Brazil's most populous states.
- **Death toll reached 64+** with dozens more missing as rescue workers searched debris.
- Towns severely affected; homes and infrastructure destroyed.
- Scientists warned that **more extreme weather events are expected** due to climate change — linked to the broader climate crisis affecting Brazilian agriculture.
- State of emergency declared in affected municipalities.

### Marielle Franco Murder — Supreme Court Sentences Killers (February 2026)
- Brazil's Supreme Court imposed **steep sentences** for the murderers of Marielle Franco, the human rights activist and city councillor assassinated in Rio de Janeiro in March 2018.
- The case became a symbol of political violence and corruption at the highest levels of Brazilian government.
- Sentences handed down to the material authors of the killing.

## 🌍 Foreign Relations

### BRICS Divided Over US–Israel Attacks on Iran (March 2026)
- As India held the BRICS chair, the bloc appeared **more cautious than usual** in responding to US-Israel strikes on Iranian nuclear facilities.
- The normally vocal anti-Western grouping showed **internal division** — particularly between China/Russia (sympathetic to Iran) and India/Brazil (more cautious/cautious).
- Questions raised about BRICS' relevance as a counterweight to Western economic dominance.

### India–Brazil Critical Minerals Deal (February 2026)
- India and Brazil signed a significant **critical minerals and rare earths agreement** — Lula called it the "core of the pioneering agreement."
- Both countries seeking to **reduce dependence on China** for critical minerals processing.
- Part of India's strategy to diversify supply chains away from China.
- Brazil holds significant rare earth deposits and is a top global producer of niobium.

### Argentina Grants Asylum to Brasilia Riot Suspect (March 2026)

- The move is likely to **sway Brazil-Argentina relations** and complicate Milei-Lula diplomatic ties.

## ☕ Agriculture & Economy

### Coffee Crisis — Climate Impacts, Historic Harvests, High Prices (March 2026)
- Brazil, as the world's largest coffee producer, facing a **paradox**: historic harvests and sky-high prices co-existing.
- Climate crisis causing unpredictable growing conditions — affecting crop quality and farmer picker availability.
- Coffee heartland (Minas Gerais) also hit by the devastating floods — agricultural sector severely impacted.

### Amazon Waterway Privatisation Blocked (February 2026)
- Local activists and river defenders successfully **stopped the privatisation** of a major Amazon waterway by occupying a grain terminal operated by a major US commodities trader.
- Victory for Amazon communities — the "river won" campaign prevented corporate control of the waterway.
