## 🇬🇧 Gibraltar — The Brexit Final Piece (March 2026)

### Gibraltar Negotiations — Spain and UK (March 2026)
- **Gibraltar** — the 6.7 sq km British Overseas Territory on Spain's southern tip — remains the last major Brexit unresolved issue.
- Spain wants **joint sovereignty with the UK** — UK wants to protect Gibraltar's British identity.
- A **draft framework agreement** exists — covering free movement, border controls, and economic cooperation.
- **Gibraltar's economy** — online gambling, financial services, and British military base (Royal Navy) — all at stake.

## 🛡️ Defence — NATO's Southern Flank (March 2026)

### Spain's Military Bases — US and NATO (March 2026)
- **Rota naval base (Cádiz)** — home to the US Navy's Sixth Fleet logistics — one of the most strategically located bases in the Mediterranean.
- **Morón Air Base (Seville)** — US drone operations and rapid deployment forces.
- Spain is **NATO's southern anchor** — the only NATO member bordering the Mediterranean with a significant military.
- Spain increasing defence budget toward **2% of NATO GDP target** — currently at ~1.5%.
